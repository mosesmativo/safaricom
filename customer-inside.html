Types of Fraud and tips to protect yourself

What is impersonation?

This is a type of fraud where the fraudster pretends to be a Safaricom employee or customer care agent.

How does it work?

The most prevalent fraud schemes are initiated via SMS and Voice calls.

The message is usually unsolicited and tends to take a similar structure to Safaricom’s or other institutions messaging. It includes genuine contacts to attempt to legitimize it. In some instances, customers will get multiple messages from the same number in the same day.

How to Report Fraud

Forward the fraudster’s number or message to 333

Reach out to us through our Official Facebook & Twitter pages on SafaricomPLC

Contact the Police on 999 or 911

What is M-PESA Global?

M-PESA global is a service that enables M-PESA registered customers to send and receive money globally. M-PESA customers will be able to send funds;

  • To East Africa: Rwanda, Tanzania & Uganda
  • Globally: To millions of Bank accounts and over 500,000 Western Union locations globally.
  • Paypal: Access funds quickly and shop around the world with PayPal mobile money service with M-PESA.

M-PESA registered customers will receive the funds from abroad on their M-PESA account.
