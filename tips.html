Tips for staying safe

  • Safaricom staff will never ask you to share your MPESA PIN, SIM, Passcodes or password
  • Notify Safaricom of imposters and suspicious branded links/websites/social media handles. Do not share any personal details with anyone on such platforms.
  • Always protect your personal information such as ID number, date of birth, full names, address etc.
  • Do not divulge any other information such as your one-time passcodes to anyone like ATM withdrawal code, authentication code for Safaricom app or other service
  • Be wary of messages from unknown senders saying you have won, or they can give you cash or help
  • Never send money to unlock prizes, loans, jobs, relief etc
  • Be wary of messages from callers who threaten you
  • Report threats to the police

What is M-PESA Global?

M-PESA global is a service that enables M-PESA registered customers to send and receive money globally. M-PESA customers will be able to send funds;

  • To East Africa: Rwanda, Tanzania & Uganda
  • Globally: To millions of Bank accounts and over 500,000 Western Union locations globally.
  • Paypal: Access funds quickly and shop around the world with PayPal mobile money service with M-PESA.

M-PESA registered customers will receive the funds from abroad on their M-PESA account.
