Introducing Baze

Video-On-Demand service that offers a wide selection of local and regional short form videos in entertainment, music, news and sports

Find Out More

Baze is a subscription based mobile-first, video-on-demand service that offers a wide selection of local and regional short form videos in entertainment, music, news and sports.

Through the platform, we are placing the entertainment Kenyans want at their fingertips while providing local content creators an additional platform to share their stories and monetize their content.

We are constantly updating our catalogue and are calling out to all Kenyan content creators across all genres to submit their original content in the following genres for consideration:

Entertainment:Comedy,Kids, Drama

Lifestyle Culture: Travel,Food, Fashion, Fitness, DIYs, Mom & Baby

Factual

News & Documentary

Sports

Faith

Entertainment:Comedy,Kids, Drama

Are you a Content Creator / Producer?

Are you a local content creator looking for a platform to showcase your work? Baze is a platform that allows you to monetize your content. Join Baze today to acquire new audiences and boost your overall revenues.

Eligibility Criteria:

  1. You must own the content and it must be original.
  2. You should have at least 1 Hour and maximum 50 Hours to be eligible as a Creator/Producer.
  3. You must be willing to be represented by a Video Content Service Provider.

What happens after you submit your application:

Once you complete the form the video content service provider will reach out to discuss further and if your content meets the requirements, they will proceed to onboard you as a Baze Content Creator.

Are you interested in becoming a Video Content Service Provider?

Baze is a platform where you can distribute your content and reach the large Safaricom customer base.

Your scope will be to:

  1. Source and aggregate local content
  2. Content evaluation and review
  3. Content ingestion: Uploading of content onto the Baze Platform.
  4. Catalogue, metadata and promotional materials preparation
  5. Marketing- You will be responsible for growing and marketing your specific content.
